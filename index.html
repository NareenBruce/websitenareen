Who I Am

About Me

I'm Nareenthiran Bhuvaneswaran, a Computer Science (Data Science) student building at the intersection of AI, data, and software. My work spans real-time computer vision, LLM-powered applications, and full-stack platforms. CGPA 3.94, Dean's List every trimester.

My Final Year Project, ARMS, automates academic peer-reviewer assignment: SPECTER transformer embeddings trained on citation graphs, matched against a self-scraped database of 493 experts and 10,817 publications, with a recency-decay function for temporal relevance and a Groq 120B RAG pipeline generating explainable justifications. Validated on a real conference dataset at 89.3% expert-level relevance, 0.4s per query.

At Juara Partners, I worked as a Data Science & AI Engineer Intern, deploying an LLM-powered WhatsApp chatbot (Docker, Meta Business API), building OCR pipelines, and engineering n8n workflows that replaced manual processes end-to-end.

Other projects: a real-time focus tracker (YOLOv8 + MediaPipe), a road hazard detection system at 75.9% mAP / 30 FPS, and a marketing prediction model deployed via Streamlit. All built on Python, FastAPI, and Next.js.

Outside of tech, I served two terms as Vice President of Student Academics at MMU's Student Representative Council, and I'm a recipient of the Yayasan Telekom Malaysia (YTM) Scholarship.
