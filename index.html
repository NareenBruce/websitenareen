Who I Am

About Me

I'm Nareenthiran Bhuvaneswaran, a Computer Science (Data Science) student at Multimedia University with a CGPA of 3.94 and Dean's List every trimester. I build things at the intersection of AI, data, and software — from real-time computer vision systems to LLM-powered applications and full-stack web platforms.

At Juara Partners, I worked as a Data Science & AI Engineer Intern, where I deployed an LLM-powered WhatsApp chatbot using Docker and the Meta Business API, built OCR pipelines, and engineered n8n automation workflows that replaced manual processes end-to-end.

For my Final Year Project, I built ARMS (Automated Reviewer Matching System) — a full-stack platform automating academic peer reviewer assignment using SPECTER transformer embeddings trained on citation graphs, matched against a self-scraped database of 493 experts and 10,817 publications. A recency decay function weights temporal relevance, and a Groq 120B LLM RAG pipeline generates explainable justifications. Validated on a real conference dataset with 89.3% expert-level relevance at 0.4s per query.

My other projects include a real-time focus tracker (YOLOv8 + MediaPipe), a road hazard detection system hitting 75.9% mAP at 30 FPS, and a marketing prediction model deployed as a Streamlit app — all built with Python, FastAPI, and Next.js.

Outside of tech, I served as Vice President of Student Academics at MMU's Student Representative Council for two consecutive terms, and I'm a proud recipient of the Yayasan Telekom Malaysia (YTM) Scholarship.
